What we brewed for this issue

In the old days, conferences used to be held once a year. But that was in the old days. In the old days, conferences used to be held either in face-to-face or remotely. But that was in the old days. In the old days, conferences used to be held only in one country. But that was in the old days.

On October 14th and 15th, Frug’Agile conference invite you to a crazy challenge… Reinforce relationship between two countries through a conference organized astride two continents, a bilingual french/english conference, a conference bringing together speakers from all over the world.

We invite you to experience other cultures and lifestyle, to support solidarity project as the Relq School, a free and inclusive coding school to give a future to young refugees.

We invite you to go back to the basics of Agile and to share all together the meaning of our agile approach.

Frug’Agile conference invite you to Frug’AgileInArmenia.
